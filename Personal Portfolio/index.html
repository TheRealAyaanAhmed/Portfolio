About Me

Hello! I'm Ayaan Ahmed, a Business Analytics major at Pace University's Lubin School of Business and a tech enthusiast based in New York City. I enjoy exploring the intersection of data, technology, and creative problem-solving, constantly seeking new ways to blend analytical thinking with innovation.

As a sophomore, I'm focused on building my skills through hands-on projects and exploring the dynamic world of finance and tech. Outside the classroom, you might find me learning new skills to grow my well-rounded mind or spending quality time with my family.

My love for learning, exploring new things, and meeting new people drives me to stay engaged across various activities. I'm involved in clubs like Alpha Beta Psi, Women in Tech, American Marketing Association, Chess Club, and the Aspire Program (Accelerated Success Professional Readiness Education). These experiences allow me to connect with diverse communities while deepening my understanding of both business and technology.
